This program is designed for drawing and interacting with geometrical shapes.

HOW TO USE:

  1. Click on the grey area three times to add three red dots.
  2. After adding the last dot you will see a parallelogram and a circle.
  3. You can change the position of the dots to see how the parallelogram and the circle get resized.
  4. Click on the "Reset" button to remove all the dots and the shapes if you want to start over again
  5. Click on the "About" button to see the description of this program.
